About me


I’m a UI/UX designer and front-end developer with a background in Information Technology and completed my MCA at Nehru College of Management.

I combine my technical expertise in frontend development—proficient in HTML, CSS with a growing proficiency in design tools like Figma. My passion for creating user-friendly and visually captivating interfaces is evident in my portfolio, which features work in wireframing, prototyping, and user research.

Continuous learning and hands-on experience, including a certification in Digital Skills: User Experience from Accenture in Future Learn, underscore my commitment to transitioning into a full-fledged UX/UI design role. I am eager to leverage my skills to contribute to innovative and user-centered design projects.
